FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I contact you?

The earlier the better — especially for weekends and summer flea markets. But don't hesitate to ask even at short notice, we'll do our best!

Do you travel to flea markets and outdoor events?

Absolutely — flea markets are our favourite! We bring our own table, chair, umbrella and all equipment. We just need a small 2×2m space.

What if it rains at an outdoor event?

We bring a portable gazebo for outdoor events. If the event is cancelled due to weather, we are happy to reschedule.

Are your paints safe for children with eczema or allergies?

We always ask about allergies before painting. We carry hypoallergenic alternatives and can do a small patch test on request. When in doubt, please consult your child's doctor first.

Do you do sensory-friendly painting?

Yes! This is something we care deeply about. We go at the child's pace, use ultra-soft brushes, offer arm and hand alternatives, and never pressure any child. Just let us know in advance so we can prepare.
