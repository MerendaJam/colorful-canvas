FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance for weekends and 1 week for weekdays. Summer and school holiday periods fill up very quickly.

Do you travel to flea markets and outdoor events?

Absolutely! Flea markets and outdoor events are our speciality. We bring our own table, chair, umbrella and all equipment — we just need a 2×2m space.

What if it rains at an outdoor event?

We bring a portable gazebo for outdoor events. If the event is cancelled due to weather, we offer free rescheduling within 3 months.

Is a deposit required?

Yes, we require a 30% deposit to secure your booking date. The remainder is payable on the day of the event.

Are your paints safe for children with eczema or allergies?

We always ask about allergies before painting. We carry hypoallergenic alternatives and can do a small patch test on request. When in doubt, please consult your child's doctor first.
