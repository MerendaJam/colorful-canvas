Pro Tip

Easy Removal with Baby Oil

When the fun is over and it's time to remove your glitter tattoo, there is no need for harsh chemicals or scrubbing. Simply follow these three easy steps:

  • Apply a small amount of baby oil directly onto the tattoo.
  • Let it soak for 30–60 seconds to loosen the body glue.
  • Gently wipe away with a soft cloth or cotton pad. Done!
💡 Note: Avoid rubbing alcohol or acetone — baby oil is all you need and it is completely gentle on skin.
