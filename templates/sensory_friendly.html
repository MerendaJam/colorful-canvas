Our Commitment

Art Without Barriers

We believe that every child — regardless of sensory needs, anxiety levels or neurodivergent conditions — deserves to experience the joy of face painting and glitter tattoos. Our sensory-friendly approach is built on patience, flexibility and genuine care.

We work with children with autism spectrum disorder (ASD), ADHD, sensory processing disorder (SPD), anxiety and other conditions. Our artist is trained in sensory-aware techniques and will always follow the child's lead.

  • No pressure — the child is always in control
  • We go at the child's own pace, however slow
  • Parents and carers are always welcome to stay close
  • We can demonstrate on a parent's hand first

Our Approach

What Makes Our Sensory Packages Different

Six key adaptations we make to ensure every child has a positive, comfortable experience.

🖌️

Ultra-Soft Brushes

We use the softest, finest brushes available — specifically chosen to minimise tactile sensitivity and discomfort on the face and skin.

🖼️

Visual Menus

Our laminated visual picture menus allow non-verbal children or those with communication differences to point and choose their design easily.

🤲

Arm & Hand Alternatives

If a child is uncomfortable with face painting, we offer the same beautiful designs on the arm, hand or wrist — same magic, different location.

⏸️

Pause & Break Friendly

We can pause at any time, take breaks, and resume when the child is ready. There is never any rush or pressure.

🔇

Quiet Space Option

At larger events, we can arrange a quieter, less stimulating area away from the main crowd for children who need a calmer environment.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family-Led Pace

Parents and carers know their child best. We always follow your guidance and welcome you to be involved throughout the entire process.

🌈

Before Your Event

We Prepare Together

For sensory-friendly bookings, we encourage a brief conversation before the event so we can prepare properly for your child's specific needs.

  • Tell us about your child's specific sensory preferences and triggers
  • Share any favourite designs or characters in advance
  • Let us know if a visual schedule or social story would help
  • We can prepare a personalised visual menu for your child
  • We can arrive early to let the child explore our setup before the event begins
