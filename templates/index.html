About Us

Passion, Creativity & a Whole Lot of Colour

We are a family from Schwetzingen who discovered a love for face painting and never looked back. What started as fun at a local flea market has grown into a true passion — bringing smiles to children and families across the Rhine-Neckar region.

We do this out of pure love for the art and for the joy on every child's face. Every design is created with care and the highest quality cosmetic-grade materials.

✓

Cosmetic-grade, FDA-approved paints — safe for even the most sensitive skin.

✓

Sensory-friendly trained — we welcome every child, no exceptions.

✓

Fast & efficient — we paint up to 20 faces per hour at peak events.

✓

Passion-driven — we do this because we love it, and it shows.
